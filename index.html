About Microsoft®:

Microsoft Corporation (/ˈmaɪkrəˌsɒft, -roʊ-, -ˌsɔːft/,[5][6] abbreviated as MS) is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Redmond, Washington, that develops, manufactures, licenses, supports and sells computer software, consumer electronics and personal computers and services. Its best known software products are the Microsoft Windows line of operating systems, Microsoft Office office suite, and Internet Explorer and Edge web browsers. Its flagship hardware products are the Xbox video game consoles and the Microsoft Surface tablet lineup. As of 2016, it is the world's largest software maker by revenue,[7] and one of the world's most valuable companies.[8]
